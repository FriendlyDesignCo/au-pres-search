Listening Sessions

In an effort to ensure that the search process is inclusive and the community is engaged, the Presidential Search Committee will hold listening sessions throughout the month of April to:

  • Answer questions and gather feedback about the search
  • Learn about the challenges and opportunities facing AU's next president
  • Discover what attributes the next president must have, in order to fulfill and move beyond the priorities of our current strategic plan
