Welcome

American University is searching for its 15th president. On March 28, Neil Kerwin announced that he would step down after more than a decade as AU's president. Under his leadership and with the combined efforts of the board and senior administration, AU has become a more transparent, academically rigorous, and fiscally sound institution.

The Board of Trustees has established a search committee with representation from students, faculty, staff, alumni, trustees, and the administration. In the coming months, the committee, aided by executive search consulting firm Spencer Stuart, will recommend one or more finalists to the board for final review and selection.

This website is designed to keep you informed about the various elements of the search process. We will post regular updates and are committed to maintaining open communication as much as possible.

We welcome your feedback at PresSearch@american.edu. You may also provide feedback directly to the committee member(s) representing your constituency.
